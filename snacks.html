Gluten-free Blueberry Muffin

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cashew meal (or blanched almond flour)
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp kal stevia (or 1/3 cup honey)
  • 1/4 tsp Himalayan salt
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 5 eggs (whipped)
  • 1 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1/4 cup canola oil

How to

  • Preheat oven to 350F.
  • Spray oil or butter a 12 muffin tin.
  • Mix the dry ingredients in a medium bowl, mix well.
  • Add canola oil to dry ingredients to incorporate, mixture will be crumbly.
  • Add vanilla extract to eggs.
  • Mix wet ingredients into cashew meal mixture.
  • Add blueberries last and mix until just incorporated.
  • Fill muffin tin 3/4 full using all the batter.
  • Cook 20-25 minutes
  • Muffins are done when toothpick comes out clean. Wait for them to cool and enjoy!!
